About Me

I am a Senior QA Engineer and QA Leader with 14+ years of experience in software quality assurance for web and mobile applications. I specialize in building effective testing strategies, leading QA teams, and driving quality initiatives that align with business goals.

My background spans both hands-on testing (functional, regression, integration) and strategic leadership, including team management, risk assessment, resource planning, and cross-functional collaboration. I have a strong track record of identifying quality risks early and working closely with Product and Engineering teams to ensure reliable, scalable releases.

As a QA leader, I focus on fostering collaboration, mentoring engineers, and continuously improving testing processes through clear metrics and modern tooling. I am particularly interested in leveraging AI and LLM-based tools to reduce repetitive work, improve test efficiency, and accelerate delivery without compromising quality.
